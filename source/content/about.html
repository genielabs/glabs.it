Hello, my name is Gene and I started to learn computers programming in early 80's when I was 12. Few years later, moved by passion not just for coding but also for tinkering, hacking and networking, I created a local BBS which then became also a FidoNet node. After that the approach into the Internet era was kind of natural but still very exciting.
Internet and programming became my work and since then I really never stopped creating software and growing up with technology.
Today I'm what they call a Full-Stack Developer with a passion for smart home, the open source world and collaborative development. Occasionally I also pretend to be learning guitar. I believe that if I keep pretending, it may accidentally truly happen at some point =)

This is where g-labs, the open source factory, came from.
